Sales Order Cancel with Reason and Reason Details

Sales Cancel Reason and Reason Details - Odoo App

This app will allow your sales user to specify reason for cancel and send email to customer for notify about cancel quote/order. Cancel Reason on Sales Order Cancel Button.

Main Features:

  • Sales order cancel with Reason and Reason details.

  • Sales cancel reason and reason details stored on sales order form.

  • Allow sales team to specify and select reason for cancelling sales order.

  • Email to customer if checkbox is ticked on wizard.
